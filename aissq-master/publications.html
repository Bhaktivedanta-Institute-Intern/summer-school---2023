Conference Proceedings

Both science and spirituality aim to understand various aspects of nature and the Ultimate Reality through diverse approaches. Science deals with physicality and attempts to explain them in a mathematical framework. Spirituality, on the other hand, entails knowledge of the self, the Supreme, and their relationship.

For the well-being of humanity, if science works by harnessing the properties of matter; spirituality, on the other hand, is a user manual for proper utilization of the gifts of God including the self. Both science and spirituality have coexisted perpetually, although the manifestation of one has been more pronounced than the other. A true scientist acknowledges the importance of spirituality, and a true spiritualist also appreciates scientific discoveries as of the “works of the God.”

At present times, the gap between science and spirituality was very much widened. It is to be bridged to bring harmony in the self, the society, the nation, and the world. All the publications mentioned below are having numerous informative & enlightening articles by World-renowned Scientists, Nobel laureates in this direction for deeper explorations of some of the fundamental questions.
